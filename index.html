Battleship "Solver"

Based on a superposition-based algorithm described here (but without the tendency to shoot every position around a hit and a little randomness to make learning the algorithm harder.)

Directions:

When you're opponent is ready, click the fire button. Ask your opponent if the coordinates indicated are a hit or a miss, and click the Ok (hit) or cancel (miss) button.

When your opponent indicates that you have sunk their X ship (which they must do), click the corresponding button. Then click the "Confirm hits" button and click on the positions on the grid where the ship was. Then click "Done".

When you have destroyed all the ships, the game is ended and the move count will be displayed.
